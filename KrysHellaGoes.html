What's your name and how can people find you on instagram?

Krys Fabriquier, @KrysHellaGoes

What and when did you get into sneaker collecting?

2006, my friends were into Nike SB and regular dunks. I was introduced to sneaker collecting as a niche.

What was the first pair in your collection and what made you get that pair?

Nike SB Classic “MC Hammer”. I went to Industrial Rideshop (RIP) looking for SBs, and they were the first things that caught my eye.

What makes your sneaker collection unique?

My sneaker collection is unique because its a reflection of me and my taste.

Hype or history and why?

Both are important. Hype helps keeps the artform progressive and continues to push the envelope. History is the foundation.

What is your grail sneaker and why?

I’ve been fortunate to own/hold a lot of my grails, but ultimately, my grail is a 1985 Metallic Air Jordan I either black or purple.

What was the best release of last year and why?

Joe Fresh Goods x New Balance 992. The color palette and use on the panels is something special. Haha.

What was your last pickup?

Stussy Huarache (Brown)

What release are you looking forward to the most this year and why?

Bacons. It was rumored for about two years and finally came into fruition.

What brands would you like to see colab this year?

Sole Collector, Converse, Mr Cartoon, Stash, Haze, Taka Hayashi, Futura2000.
