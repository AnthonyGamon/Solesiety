What's your name and how can people find you on instagram?

Hi Everyone! My name is Anthony but you know me as Staytrillbruh on Instagram.

What and when did you get into sneaker collecting?

I got into sneaker collecting because I knew a few other people who were collecting and I had always loved the Tiffany dunk sb's. I've been collecting for almost a decade now.

What was the first pair in your collection and what made you get that pair?

My first pair of sneakers I purchased were actually a pair of 2011 Black Cement 3's. I just really liked how that shoe looked and looked good with really anything.

What makes your sneaker collection unique?

One cool thing about my collection is I own almost every Black Cement 3 produced. I am only missing the 1988 pair.

Hype or history and why?

I think a few years back I think sneaker collecting revolved around history and since it has shifted to hype. But I think both play an important role in sneaker collecting as time goes.

What is your grail sneaker and why?

My current grail is probably the Nike Blink Yeezy 1.
