What's your name and how can people find you on instagram?

Noah Albert @njalbert._

What and when did you get into sneaker collecting?

I had always appreciated sneakers throughout my life, but in 7th grade the first pair of NICE sneakers I bought was a pair of French Blue 12’s. Since then sneaker fever has ran though my bones.

What was the first pair in your collection and what made you get that pair?

French Blue 12’s were the first pair I bought once I started collecting. I bought this shoe because I loved the way the blue and white compliment each other and I loved the touches of red on the sole

What makes your sneaker collection unique?

My sneaker collection is unique because it is composed of all MY favorite pairs. While there are pairs that are considered “hype” in my collection that’s not why I purchased them. Every shoe I own because I loved the way they look and I love the story behind them

Hype or history and why?

History for sure. And not just the history of the shoe/color way but the story of how the shoe came into my possession and who all owned it before. We’re all one big community and stories like that connect us all

What is your grail sneaker and why?

My grail is the Chicken and Waffle Nike SB Dunk. I remember seeing this shoe in the mall when I was younger but didn’t have the money to buy it at the time. I have regretted pulling the trigger on it ever sense

What was the best release of last year and why?

I think the Black nylon Sacai Waffle racer was the best sneaker released last year. It is such a clean and simple shoe yet still so elegant. Quite the underrated color way of this sneaker

What was your last pickup?

My latest pick up was a pair OU Jordan 9’s this pair was my other grail that I was lucky enough to be gifted a pair of.

What release are you looking forward to the most this year and why?

I’m really looking forward to the Cool Grey Jordan 11’s coming out this Holliday season. I’ve been waiting a long time for this shoe to retro again

What brands would you like to see colab this year?

I would love to see a Chinatown Market and Nike shoe colab. Chinatown is my favorite streetwear brand and I think they deserve more attention than they get.
