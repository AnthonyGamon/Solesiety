What and when did you get into sneaker collecting?

As a kid I always had dope shoes thanks to my parents and grandparents, but the older I got I knew I wanted to have dope shoes. And once I finally started making money to consistently cop sneakers I made it happen.

Hate to be basic and obvious, but I would say the Low Top Air Force One. I had the white pair with the blue swoosh! I think it was an anniversary special...but that was my first shoe I bought with my own money. AF1’s were the wave back then and that was my first pair!
